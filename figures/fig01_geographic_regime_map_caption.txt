Figure 1. Geographic distribution of contamination decay regimes identified by Self-Organizing Map clustering across 138,768 mesh cells in the Fukushima region. Each point represents a 250-m grid cell colored by its assigned regime. The black triangle marks the Fukushima Daiichi Nuclear Power Station (FDNPP). Regimes were determined by hierarchical clustering (Ward's D2, k=6) of a 5x5 hexagonal SOM trained on 9 temporal features.
